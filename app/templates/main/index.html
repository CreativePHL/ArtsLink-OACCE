ABOUT

ArtsLink is free web tool that helps neighborhood programs and facilities discover high quality arts and cultural experiences for their communities. The goal of ArtsLink is to promote community connectivity and wellbeing through increased arts education and instruction opportunities in neighborhoods throughout Philadelphia.

=======

Who Is It For?

ArtsLink is a free resource for Philadelphians to discover arts programs from local organizations that best fit their needs or the needs of their community.
