ArtsLink: A tool to discover Philadelphia's arts programs

ArtsLink is a free web tool to help Philadelphians find quality arts experiences in neighborhoods across the city.

Click Here To Browse or Search for Programs

Who Is It For?

ArtsLink is a free resource for Philadelphians to discover arts programs from local organizations that best fit their needs or the needs of their community.

How to Find an Arts Program

Click to browse. Looking for opportunities for a specific art type (e.g. theater/drama, dance, music performance, etc.), art activity (e.g. art making, field trips, etc.), or time (e.g. in school, after school, summer, etc.)? Filter to find the arts program(s) that best fit your needs.

How to Submit Your Arts Program

Click to register. Create an account to promote your organization and the arts programs you offer!
